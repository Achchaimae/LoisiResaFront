Program

Morning:

  1. 8:00 AM - Arrival and Breakfast:

    Members gather at the club. Enjoy a hearty breakfast to fuel up for the day's activities.

  2. 9:00 AM - Horseback Riding in the Forest:

    Prepare the horses and riders for a scenic ride through the forest trails. Explore nature and enjoy the tranquility of the surroundings.

  3. 11:00 AM - Return to Horse Stable:

    Safely return to the horse stable after the forest ride.

Midday:

  1. 11:30 AM - Horse Grooming Session:

    Learn and practice proper horse care techniques. Brush and groom the horses, fostering a bond between riders and their mounts.

  2. 12:30 PM - Picnic Lunch in the Fields:

    Pack a delicious picnic lunch. Set off on horseback to picturesque fields. Enjoy lunch amidst the natural beauty, surrounded by fields and rolling hills.

Afternoon:

  1. 2:30 PM - Visit to the Beach:

    Embark on a leisurely ride to the nearby beach. Experience the serenity of the coastline and the sound of waves crashing. Take in breathtaking views and enjoy the fresh sea breeze.

  2. 4:30 PM - Sunset Watching:

    Find a cozy spot on the beach to watch the mesmerizing sunset. Reflect on the day's adventures and bond with fellow riders. Capture unforgettable memories against the backdrop of the setting sun.

Evening:

  1. 6:00 PM - Return to the Club:

    Safely return to the horse riding club after a day of exploration and relaxation.
